What Each Phase Actually Does

Setup — LD-01 to LD-07

Complete Project Configuration

  • Shooter Profile: Skill level, discipline (Benchrest/PRS/F-Class/Hunting/ELR), experience, MOA/ES/SD targets, equipment inventory
  • Rifle Configuration: Full specs including barrel length, twist rate, contour, material, trigger pull weight, optic details, serial number, round count, and rifle photo
  • Caliber & Platform: SAAMI/CIP spec sheet with 20+ cartridge dimensions auto-loaded
  • Component Selection: Brass (brand, lot, quantity), Primer (type/size/sensitivity), Bullet (weight, diameter, type, G1/G7 BC, sectional density), Powder (burn rate, type, suitable calibers)
Brass Prep — LD-08 to LD-14

Inspection Through Neck Tension

  • Brass Inspection: Weight sorting guidance, 7-point defect checklist (mouth cracks, body dents, head stamps, expansion, internal cracks, pocket looseness, corrosion)
  • Chamber Measurement: COAL/CBTO to lands, multiple measurement methods, freebore calculation, comparator datum, baseline tracking
  • Trim & Chamfer: Trim length, neck turning decision/tracking, target wall thickness, post-turn average
  • Case Sizing: Method (FL/Neck/Bushing), shoulder bump measurement, bushing size, die settings
  • Neck Tension: Target tension, bushing selection, loaded neck OD measurement
Planning — LD-15 to LD-17

Strategy & Ladder Design

  • Seating Depth Strategy: Jump distance planning based on your chamber measurements, integrates with BallisticPlotter coarse sweep generation
  • Pressure Baseline: Safe starting & maximum charge weights from published data
  • Charge Ladder Plan: Centered Middle-10 algorithm generates all charges in 0.2gr increments, selects the optimal middle 10 — your test plan is built automatically
Testing — LD-18 to LD-21

Range Day Data Capture

  • Velocity Testing: Per-shot chrono data with full environmental conditions (temp, wind, humidity, altitude, barometric pressure, density altitude, mirage)
  • Accuracy Testing: Group size (inches & MOA), vertical/horizontal spread, mean radius, POI offset, target photos, flyer tracking, confidence ratings
  • Pressure Evaluation: Comprehensive checklist — primer signs (cratering, flattening, flow, pierced, blown), case signs (ejector marks, head expansion, web bulging, sticky extraction, bolt lift), velocity anomalies, severity assessment
Optimization — LD-22 to LD-27

BallisticPlotter-Driven Decisions

  • Select Optimal Charge: BallisticPlotter pre-fills the recommended charge weight, 2 alternates, and a plain-English rationale explaining why
  • Seating Depth Fine Tune: Two-phase optimization — coarse sweep (0.010" increments) then fine sweep (0.002" centered on best coarse result)
  • Extended Group Testing: 20-30 shot validation groups for statistical confidence, loops until scoring engine confirms
  • Consistency Verification: ES/SD verification against your target thresholds
Final & Advanced — LD-28 to LD-33

Certification & Long-Term Tracking

  • Final Load Certification: Formal sign-off with complete data review across all phases
  • Load Documentation: Full recipe export — every component, measurement, and performance data point
  • Batch Planning: Production batch calculator with component needs and cost estimation
  • Barrel Life Tracking: Round count, throat erosion monitoring (baseline vs. current COAL-to-lands), accuracy degradation detection, cleaning schedule, barrel condition assessment
  • Component Change Monitoring: Lot change tracking and performance delta logging

BallisticAnalysis™

Two-Point Scale Calibration

Tap two points on any target at a known distance apart. The system calculates a precise pixels-per-inch scale factor, converting your phone camera into a measurement instrument. Full pinch-to-zoom with InteractiveViewer for sub-pixel placement.

Precision Shot Marking

Tap to place shot impacts rendered at actual bullet-diameter scale (calibrated to your cartridge). Drag to reposition with a magnifier loupe for fine adjustment. Undo/redo support. Each shot is tracked as X/Y coordinates in real-world inches relative to calibration.

Complete Statistical Engine

Every group is analyzed with 14+ measurements: Group Size (Extreme Spread CC), MOA, MIL, Mean Radius, CEP (Circular Error Probable — 50% hit probability radius), Radial SD, Horizontal SD, Vertical SD, Horizontal Spread, Vertical Spread, Group Centroid (X,Y), POA Offset (windage & elevation — tells you exactly how far to adjust your scope), and a Quality Score.

POA Offset — Scope Adjustment Data

Computes windage (X) and elevation (Y) offset from your Point of Aim. Know exactly how many clicks to adjust before your next group — not a guess, a measurement.

Branded Share Images

Generates full iPhone Pro Max resolution (1290×2796) share images with annotated target overlay, shot circles, extreme spread line, statistics box, and Choice Tactical branding. Ready for social media, match records, or your reloading journal.

BallisticPlotter™

Deterministic Scoring Engine — No AI Guessing, No Black Box

5-Component Weighted Scoring Formula

Every charge group gets a 0–100 composite score using locked, transparent weights. Vertical dispersion is 40% because it's the #1 predictor of a true node. No machine learning, no hidden variables — you can verify every score yourself.

Charge Node Ranking with Reason Codes

The engine doesn't just say "this charge is best" — it tells you why. Each ranked candidate is annotated with specific codes: BEST_VERTICAL, TIGHT_GROUP, GOOD_CHRONO, ROUND_GROUP. Runner-ups show BETTER_VERTICAL_THAN_WINNER or BETTER_CHRONO_THAN_WINNER so you understand the trade-offs.

Two-Phase Seating Depth Optimization

Coarse sweep: 5 offsets at 0.010" increments. Engine identifies the best zone. Fine sweep: 5 offsets at 0.002" centered on the best coarse result. Measurement-agnostic — uses thousandths offset from baseline, works with COAL or CBTO.

Chrono Spike Detection

Flags abnormal velocity jumps >50 fps between adjacent charge weights with specific warnings like “CHRONO_SPIKE_41.0_TO_41.5_DELTA_65FPS” — potential pressure indicators that demand attention before continuing.

Automatic Workflow Routing

Based on confidence score and warning flags, the engine routes PrecisionLoad to the exact right next step: pressure evaluation if warnings detected, charge selection if confidence ≥75%, or extended testing if more data is needed. Pre-fills the next screen with recommended charges, alternates, and rationale.

The Scoring Formula — Transparent & Locked

Each component uses inverse-proportion scoring: 100 / (1 + value × k). Smaller measurements = higher scores.

Component Weight What It Measures Why It Matters
Vertical Dispersion 40% verticalInches The #1 predictor of a true charge node — vertical stringing means velocity variation
Group Size 25% groupSizeInches (extreme spread CC) Overall group tightness — what most shooters measure at the range
Mean Radius 15% meanRadiusInches Average tightness from centroid — less sensitive to single outliers than ES
Geometry Quality 10% min(H,V) / max(H,V) Group roundness — 1.0 = perfect circle, low = stringing in one axis
Chrono Support 10% SD (fps) & ES (fps) Velocity consistency — neutral if no chrono data (never penalizes missing data)

3-Factor Confidence Score — How Certain Is the Decision?

0% — Re-test 75% — Threshold 100%
Three weighted factors:

60% — Absolute quality of top candidate's composite score

30% — Separation gap between #1 and #2 (clear winner = high confidence)

10% — Data completeness (missing groups, low marking confidence penalized)

Deterministic Routing — Where Does It Send You?

After Accuracy Testing (LD-20)

Pressure warnings detected → Pressure Evaluation (LD-21). Confidence ≥75% → Select Optimal Charge (LD-22). Otherwise → Extended Group Testing (LD-25) with top 3 candidates and recommended shot count.

After Extended Testing (LD-25)

Confidence ≥75% → Select Optimal Charge (LD-22) with pre-filled recommendation. Otherwise → Stay on LD-25, tighten test with refined candidates and increased shot count (20→30).

After Seating Depth Coarse (LD-23)

Always loops back to LD-23 for fine sweep — 5 offsets at 0.002" increments centered on the best coarse offset.

After Seating Depth Fine (LD-23)

Confidence ≥75% → Extended Group Testing (LD-25) with refined seating depth. Otherwise → Re-test LD-23 with shifted baseline.
